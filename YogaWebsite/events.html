Upcoming Classes and Events

Hatha Yoga
8:00AM to 9:00AM

+ More Info

Hatha yoga is a practice of channeling the bodies energies to balance the body and mind. The techniques offered in this style often focus on the use of postures and breathing techniques to reach this equilibrium. Expect a beginner experience that will help learn basics for future yoga courses.

Slow Flow Yoga
10:00AM to 11:00AM

+ More Info

Slow Flow yoga is exactly as it sounds, a series of slow fluid postures and sequences of movements. This class places an emphasis on holding poses longer and focusing more on movement and breath, flowing from one set of postures to another. Beginners are welcome and encouraged, but some sets of movements can be vigorous and demanding.

Gentle Yoga
8:00AM to 9:00AM

+ More Info

Gentle yoga is a slower paced, restorative yoga meant to relax the body and reduce stress. Including less intense positions including time for meditation and breath work. Comparative to an easier Hatha, this class is also great for beginners.

Chair Yoga
3:00PM to 4:00PM

+ More Info

Another style that matches its name, chair yoga is an adaptation of different movements and poses that can be done while seated. Chair yoga can help increase mobility, increase blood flow, reduce stress, stretch the body, and clear the mind. These course are welcome to all and are especially created for the elderly and those with physical disability.

Deep Stretch Yoga
10:00AM to 11:00AM

+ More Info

Deep stretch focuses on releasing tight muscles and tendons, while reducing stress on the skeletal structure. These classes target muscle groups in the hips, hamstrings, and upper back. These course can be highly intensive for core work and are targeted more towards intermediate or more experienced Yogi.

Restorative Yoga
5:00PM to 6:00PM

+ More Info

Restorative yoga is a restful practice that involves passively holding poses and focusing on breathing to help relieve tension from your body. Relaxing the mind and body in these course helps the body rest, heal, and restore balance. This class is very helpful for beginners and advanced Yogi alike.
